Why Use HubCap?

Expand Your Market

HubCap puts a traditional service into the hands of a modern day person. With a dedicated app, people are more likely to seek out and use car services.

No Upfront Costs

There are no upfront costs to use our vendor suite. Sign up and try it out. If you find it's not for your business, don't sweat it.

Streamline Core Administrative Duties

Updating packages, creating new promotions, modifying store hours, scheduling appointments, and more can all be done in one place with less hassle.

Simplify Checkout and Payment

Faster checkout means faster turnover. Purchases made with QR codes means customers can checkout without ever getting out of their vehicle.
