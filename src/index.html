Photography

My interest in photography and photo editing developed from an unlikely source; a video game. Halo 3 came out in 2007 and featured what was called a Theater Mode that allowed players to rewatch their gameplay experiences and, of course, take pictures. Eventually, I began to explore photo and video editing, uploading photos to my home computer and editing them with Photoshop. From there, I started using whatever camera I could get my hands on until I was able to afford my own.


I've taken thousands of pictures since my initial interest back in 2007 and have selected a few albums to share here. You'll find my recently accumulated work ranging from intimate nature shots, to candid views of a bustling New York City, to some more professional work.
