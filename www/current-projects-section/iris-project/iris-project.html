Iris
A chat/media sharing application intended to build on Google and Facebook's chat applications

My friends and I have recently expressed our frustration with some of the popular chat services we use like Google Hangouts and Facebook messenger. Our gripe was the lack of any organization elements or functionality. We create a lot of group chats to make plans or share content and find that most of it ends up getting buried deep in a conversation thread quickly. With some of us working as professional developers now, we decided that it was time to put our heads together and create something of our own.

Concept

The most powerful feature that set our chat app apart from the others was a tagging system that allowed users to link content (pictures, files, links, etc.) to a group made for a respective conversation. This allowed the group to post and refer to content discussed in the conversation thread seamlessly. The mockups posted here, while incomplete, begin to demonstrate how some of that would work.
