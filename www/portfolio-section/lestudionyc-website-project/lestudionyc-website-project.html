LeStudioNYC
A professional portrait and headshot photography site

Le Studio NYC is located in historic DUMBO Brooklyn, NYC and produces some of the most impressive portrait photography in the NYC area. Run by the professional photographer and artist, Laetitia Soulier, her creative talent and beautiful studio are sure to please anyone fortunate enough to know about her. You can check out her new site at LeStudioNYC.com.

I worked side by side with Laetitia when developing the site, ensuring that every last detail of the design and functionality was to a tee. The objective was to create a more robust and responsive site with booking, contact-form, and blog functionality while still maintaining the minimal look of her old site. Additionally, a new slider and gallery were implemented to offer smoother interaction and responsive design.
