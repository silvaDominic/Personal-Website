How it works

Search or use your own location to find a list of local washers and detailers around you.

Browse through stores quickly with easy to read wash package menus.

Take a deeper look at each store to find advanced pricing options, promotions, and reviews.

Tap the price button to automatically add a package to your cart.

View your cart to add, remove, or edit existing packages.

When you arrive at your selected car wash you'll be prompted to scan your device. Simply select your package, tap activate, and scan. That's it!
