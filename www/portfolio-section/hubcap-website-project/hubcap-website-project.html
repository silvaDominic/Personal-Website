Hubcap
The promotional website for the car wash comparison company, Hubcap

HubCap is a new start-up whose services currently focus on aggregating prices and metadata on car washes. Not only will the app provide a platform for car washes but it will also streamline the process of setting up, searching, purchasing, and using them by offering simple and uniform menu systems. You can find out how it all works at HubcapCare.com

As a co-founder and web developer & designer for the company, I was tasked with designing the promotional site for the company. I wanted to make the site as simple and light as possible while still being able to demonstrate how the service works for both customers and vendors. Given that the site is likely the entry point for new users, I carried over much of the color palette from the actual app to familiarize the brand.
