Barbers Society
A minimal site for a sophisticated, yet casual barbershop

Sticking with the minimal design of the shop itself, the site small with only a few pages and standard features such as a blog page, a latest post section, and an easy to access booking section. Built using Wordpress and the powerful Beaver Page Builder, the site was put together in a snap and I was able to rest easy knowing that the owners will have no problem managing or making modifications on their own.

Barbers Society is a relatively new barbershop located in Soho, NYC that holds Italian values close to its practice. Offering premium haircuts, shaves, shampoos, and even whisky, this niche barbershop aims to make a statement on a tradition with a deep history. Check out the site and schedule an appointment with one of their professional Italian barbers at BarbersSociety.com
